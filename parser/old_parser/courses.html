Here you can see which courses are offered at the IT University of Copenhagen.

Please note that the courses outlined below can contain courses, that are in preparation and thus not necessarily will end up as part of the final course offer. Please note also that schedule information for such courses may be either lacking or tentative. You can see whether a course is being prepared by checking whether the course title is a link. If the course title is NOT a link, the course is being prepared.
